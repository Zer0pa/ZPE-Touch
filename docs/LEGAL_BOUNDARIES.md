# Legal Boundaries

Scope boundaries matter on the legal surface as well as the technical surface:

- this repo is distributed under `Zer0pa Source-Available License v7.0`; see `LICENSE`
- this repo publishes bounded touch encoding only
- affective touch is out of scope
- the public claim is limited to contact, thermal, vibrotactile, and proprioceptive adopters exactly as documented here
- proof artifacts describe the committed evidence surface; they do not expand the claim by implication
